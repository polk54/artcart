Frequently Asked Questions

The short version

  • No account, no tracking, no ads. Your work stays on your device and in your own iCloud.
  • 3 projects free. ArtCart Unlimited removes the limit for $9.99/year or $1/month.
  • Cancel anytime — you keep everything you made; you just can't add new projects until you subscribe again.

Privacy & your data

Is my data private? Can you see my projects?

Nobody but you can see your work. ArtCart has no servers and no accounts, so there's nowhere for the developer to see anything. Your projects, photos, notes, timelines, and boards stay on your device, and (if you use iCloud) in your own private iCloud. There's no analytics, no tracking, and no ads. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Where is my data stored? Does it sync across devices?

By default, everything lives on your device. If you're signed in to iCloud with it enabled for ArtCart, your data also syncs through your own private iCloud across your devices and is included in your iCloud backups. This is handled entirely by Apple. Only you can access it.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up, no email, and no password. Open the app and start making.

Pricing

What's free, and what does ArtCart Unlimited cost?

You can create up to 3 projects for free, with every feature included: notes, supplies, links, the progress timeline, the drawing board, and the dice. ArtCart Unlimited removes the project limit for $9.99/year or $1/month.

What happens if I cancel, or my subscription ends?

You keep every project you've made. Nothing is deleted, hidden, or locked. The only change is that you can't create new projects beyond three until you subscribe again. Everything you've already built stays fully visible and editable. Your subscription auto-renews until you cancel; you can manage or cancel anytime in Settings → your name → Subscriptions (or the App Store account menu).

How do I redeem a code?

If you have an offer code open the subscribe screen in ArtCart and tap Redeem, or go to the App Store → your account → Redeem Gift Card or Code.

Using ArtCart

Which crafts is it for?

Any of them. Knitting, embroidery, jewelry-making, quilting, wood-working, painting, etc. You pick the craft type for each project, and catalog it in the way that works best for you. If you feel like we're missing a category - reach out!

Can I use it on more than one device?

Yes, your projects sync across your iPhones through iCloud, so they're there on any iPhone signed in to the same iCloud account. ArtCart is currently designed for iPhone.
