Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

ArtCart is built to keep your creative work yours. It's an inventory-first tracker for your craft projects — and it's designed so your projects, photos, and notes stay private by default. Here's exactly what that means.

The short version

  • Your data lives on your device and, if you use iCloud, in your own private iCloud.
  • No account to create — you just start making.
  • No tracking, no analytics, no ads, no third-party data sharing.
  • We have no servers and cannot see your projects.

Where your data lives

Everything you create in ArtCart — projects, photos, notes, timelines, and boards — is stored locally on your device.

If you're signed in to iCloud and have iCloud enabled for ArtCart, your data also syncs through Apple's iCloud to your own private iCloud account, so it stays in sync across your devices and is included in your iCloud backups. This sync is handled entirely by Apple. Your content is stored in your private iCloud database, which only you can access — the developer cannot see it, and it is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

If you're not signed in to iCloud, ArtCart simply keeps your data on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. ArtCart does not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any developer-run server, because there are no developer-run servers. We don't use analytics, advertising, crash-tracking, or any third-party SDKs that would gather information about you or how you use the app.

Photos

When you add a photo to a project or board, it's saved as part of ArtCart's own data on your device (and to your private iCloud if sync is on). ArtCart only accesses the specific photos you choose to add, and it doesn't upload them anywhere other than your own iCloud.

Third parties

ArtCart uses no third-party analytics or advertising services. The only external service involved is Apple iCloud, and only when you've enabled it — for syncing your own data to your own account.

If a future version of ArtCart adds optional features such as subscriptions, any new services involved (for example, Apple's App Store for payments) will be described in an updated version of this policy before they're used.

Children's privacy

ArtCart is a personal project tracker and is not directed at children under 13. Because the app collects no personal information, it doesn't knowingly gather data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update it here and revise the date at the top. Meaningful changes will be reflected before any new behavior takes effect.
